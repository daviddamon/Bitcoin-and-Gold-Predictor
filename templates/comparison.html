The chart below compares the growth rate of Bitcoin, Gold, and the S&P 500 Index. Some of the main takeaways, which are evident in the chart below are:

  • Bitcoin has seen tremendous growth in recent years, but is prone to wild fluctuations for a variety of reasons.
  • The price of gold has not increased appreciably from 10 year ago.
  • The S&P 500 Index, a broad indicator of the market, has increased steadily over this period.

Some factors to consider that affect the price of both Bitcoin and gold are:

  • Both Bitcoin and Gold have a relatively stable supply, with demand and investor behavior being the main price drivers.
  • Gold is used to diversify investments during periods of stock market volitility, which in turn causes the price of gold to increase.
