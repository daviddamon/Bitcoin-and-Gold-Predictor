Welcome to Our Project

Our project uses machine learning to predict Bitcoin worth using the previous day's values. We designed our application hoping it would be helpful for someone trying to evaluate bitcoin for their own finance or for someone just trying to understand bitcoin. For that reason, we have included information about gold as a base currency comparison with which most users should be familiar, despite its vast differences from cryptocurrency. We also provide some basic information about how bitcoin came to be and how it is doing today via some of the most popular articles in the news, curated on our news page. Feel free to check out our pages for bitcoin in the news, a bit of bitcoin history, comparison against gold's worth, and for more information on our developers.

About Our Process

We created this web application as a powerful tool to minimize risk for someone who is looking to invest in Bitcoin. This application will allow you to predict the end price of Bitcoin for the day based on the High and Low. We are able to make these predictions used out ML models that have been trained on the past data to give as accurate a prediction as possible. We chose to use the previous 30 days data to predict because we realized the outdated data tended to skew our model and yielded less accurate results. We chose tensorflow as opposed to scikit-learn because of we we found that tensorflow allowed us to fine tune our model to minimize loss as much as possible.The we used a python function to load it into the flask app because it was easier than trying to use pickle.
