What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin at its core is a decentralized cryptocurrency. Every single Bitcoin transaction that took place and ever will take place is listed on something called the "open ledger." This is what makes Bitcoin so secure and can help explain its recent rise in popularity among consumers. The ledger is protected by mining machines that monitor transactions. The transactions are then loaded into items called nodes and once the nodes are all in agreement, the transaction is returned to the miners. According to multiple articles (just Google explain Bitcoin like I'm five), Bitcoin is about 400,000 times more powerful than the most powerful supercomputer.
